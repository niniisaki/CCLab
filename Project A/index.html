Family:

Florpians are not sociable creatures, a young Florp stays with their parents up until age 6, but until then they are taught how to survive, culture, and culture by their parents. Though Florpians tend to have strong loving emotions towards family members, they tend to leave their familes in hopes to find their soulmate. But they prefer to stay in solitude while searching for their mate.

Habitat:

Florpians are born in darkness, they prefer empty spaces. They live in the world between the void and deep ocean. Not much is know about their habitat.

Appearance:

Florpians main characteristics is actually their eyes and ears. Their eyes are gigantic and far apart creating a cute but humourous Appearance. Their ears, are actually antennae, used to communicate with their own kind as well as others, but they tend to vary in appearances. Most Florpians can only change up to two colors in their lifespan, but this specific Florp appears to have the ability to change to many colors.

Key Characteristics:

  • Floats around endlessly, going from one side to another.
  • Can use their antennae to hypnotize or manipulate people as well as project their thoughts onto others .
  • Keep an eye on them, they can disappear at times and reappear in random places

What we know so far about Florpians? Florpians are theorized to be a creature that is a cross between oceanic and alien-like. Like stated earlier, Florpians branch off from their parents around the age of 6 after learning survival skills, but many scientist have hypothesized that they are able to keep in contact with their family through their antennae. Florpians come in a range of personality types, this one specifically tends to be very mischievous, but they all share one instinct, the search for their soulmate, they yearn for their soulmate dearly until they are able to come across their beloved.
